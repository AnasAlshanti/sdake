About Us

What is the definition of an association
An independent charitable non-governmental association dedicated to alleviating poverty and suffering of Palestinian people in need, through the implementation of several charitable and development projects, as well as supporting young people and their intellectual, cultural and social advancement, in order to empower them, create jobs for them, and enhance their role in society.
What is the message of the association
Provide all services available to our people (social - living - health - recreational - educational.. etc.) to alleviate their suffering and ensure a decent life.
What is the Society's Vision
We look forward to serving our people, adopting their causes and defending them in order to alleviate their suffering and ensure a decent life for them as active persons. The Association contributes to the sustainable development of society under the tender of all its slices and children.
What are the objectives of the Society
- Ensuring orphans' and poor families and their needs and providing services to the wounded, the sick and those with special needs. - Provision of cash and emergency assistance and in-kind assistance to reluctant families.
